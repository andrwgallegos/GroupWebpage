DXA Mission & Team Rules

Mission Statement

Team DXA is committed to creating work that is thoughtful, functional, and visually engaging. We value adaptability, communication, respect, and consistent effort. As computer scientists, we aim to learn continuously, support one another, and build solutions that reflect both technical skill and creativity.

Team Identity

  • We value communication and accountability.
  • We approach problems with creativity and logic.
  • We support each other’s growth and contributions.
  • We want our work to be both functional and polished.

Team Rules

  • Respect everyone’s ideas and time.
  • Communicate updates early and clearly.
  • Complete assigned tasks by agreed deadlines.
  • Ask for help when blocked instead of going silent.
  • Keep contributions organized for the rest of the team.
  • Encourage improvement without putting others down.

  • Andrew: Coordinator / Content Integration
